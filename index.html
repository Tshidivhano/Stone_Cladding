About Stone Cladding

Stone Cladding is a proudly South African company based in Mooi River. We specialize in natural stone construction, with a passion for blending timeless design with structural integrity.

With over 10 years of hands-on experience in the stonework industry, we've become trusted providers for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects across the nation.

Our Mission

To enhance the built environment using nature's finest materials. We strive to deliver excellent workmanship, promote sustainable practices, and help our clients fall in love with their spaces all over again.
